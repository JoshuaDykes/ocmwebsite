Frequently Asked Questions

What are your visiting hours?

Our administrative offices are open Monday through Friday from 9am to 5pm. Visits to our facilities require advance scheduling. Please contact us to arrange a visit to our school or orphanage.

How can I volunteer with OCM?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities, from administrative support to teaching assistance, facility maintenance, and special events. Please fill out our contact form selecting "Volunteer Opportunity" or visit our Get Involved page for more information.

Can I sponsor a specific child?

Yes, our child sponsorship program allows you to support a specific child's education, nutrition, and healthcare. You'll receive updates about your sponsored child's progress. Visit our Sponsor a Child page to learn more.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

Yes, we accept donations of clothing, school supplies, books, toys, and other items that support our children's needs. Please contact us before bringing donations to ensure we can use the items you wish to donate.

How can I support from outside the country?

We welcome international support through online donations, sponsorship programs, and partnership opportunities. Visit our Donation page for information on international payment methods.
