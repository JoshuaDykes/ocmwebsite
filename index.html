Our Mission

Overcomers Caring Ministries exists to uplift and empower vulnerable and needy children in our community through the love of Christ. We provide a safe and nurturing environment with our school, home, and orphanage, focusing on education, basic needs, and talent development. We believe every child deserves a chance to thrive.
