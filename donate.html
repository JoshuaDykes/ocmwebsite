Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Overcomers Caring Ministries is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a tax receipt for your records.

How is my donation used?

Your donation directly supports our programs for vulnerable children, including education, nutrition, healthcare, shelter, and emotional support. We strive to maintain low administrative costs, with over 85% of donations going directly to our programs.

Can I cancel my monthly donation?

Yes, you can cancel your monthly donation at any time by contacting our donor support team at ministryovercomers@gmail.com or by calling +254724406285.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your personal and payment information. We never store your full credit card details on our servers.

Can I donate by check or bank transfer?

Yes, you can mail a check to our office at Ongata Rongai, Nairobi, Kenya. For bank transfers, please contact our finance team at ministryovercomers@gmail.com for our banking details.
